Crockpot Chili

Homepage

I got this from my dad, who got it from the back of a McCormic mix.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a diced red pepper
  • 1/4 white onion
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can hot chili beans
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1lb ground beef
  • McCormic hot chili seasoning packet
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic salt

Directions

With a drizzle of oil fry the redd pepper with the onion until softened.

Add the beef in and cook through. Add seasoning packet, garlic salt, and pepper.

In the crockpot add the beans and tomatoes as well as the beef mixture and as much cayenne pepper as you like. Cook on high for four hours. Serve with sour cream and shredded cheese.
