Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 1 lemon
  • 1lb ground beef
  • spices of your choice
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sour cream
  • 2 pitas
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • Ketchup
  • (optional) Golden raisins

Directions

Cut half of the onion into thin slices, and quarter the lemon.

Mix the onion in a small bowl with juice from half of the lemon and a pinch of salt. Microwave for 30-40 seconds and stir it. Set it aside and let it pickle

Cook the beef with a squirt of ketchup, optional raisins, spices, salt and pepper.

Wrap the pitas in moist paper towels and microwave them for 30-40 seconds. cut them in half and split them down the middle as is in the picture.

Fill the pitas with beef, onions and hot sauce and enjoy
